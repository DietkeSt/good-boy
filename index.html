Instructions on How To Play

  1. Please enter your Name.
  2. Click on the Start Quiz button.
  3. Guess the correct dog breed by clicking on the option with the correct Breed Name.
  4. Click the Next button.
  5. Repeat until you see the Results button.
  6. Click Results to see your final score!
  7. You can retake the quiz anytime by clicking the Retake button.

Hint: You can click on the images to enlarge them for a better view. Clicking on the enlarged image again will lead you back to the quiz.

Are you sure you want to re-take the quiz?

All progress will be lost upon re-taking the quiz and you will need to enter your name again to start the quiz from the beginning.
